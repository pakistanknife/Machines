Can I see the machines before buying?

Yes. We are located at Shahpura Road Industrial Area, Sialkot, Pakistan, and welcome on-site visits by appointment. For buyers who cannot visit, we provide live video walk-throughs and demonstrations on WhatsApp before any commitment.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We have completed multiple international shipments. We coordinate with established freight forwarders at Karachi port for sea-container shipment to anywhere in the world. Shipping costs and lead time depend on destination — WhatsApp us for an exact quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

For domestic Pakistani buyers: bank transfer or cash on collection. For international buyers: telegraphic transfer (TT) is most common, typically with a deposit before shipment and the balance against shipping documents. Details on WhatsApp.
