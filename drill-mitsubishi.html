About this 5 Heads Drill Machine (Mitsubishi)

A multi-spindle drill (also called multi-head drill, gang drill, or cluster drill) is a machine where several drill spindles operate in parallel, allowing multiple holes to be drilled in a single cycle. This unit has 5 heads — each cycle produces 5 holes, a major productivity gain over a single-spindle drill press for any part with a repeated hole pattern.

The Mitsubishi badge matters here. Mitsubishi industrial drilling machines are known worldwide for their build quality, spindle bearing life, and longevity.
