About this 1000 Amp Rectifier

An industrial DC rectifier converts AC mains supply into the steady, controlled direct current needed for electroplating, anodizing, electroforming, electrowinning, and electrolysis. The 1000-ampere rating places this unit in the medium-large industrial class — sufficient for a serious plating line running cutlery, surgical instruments, automotive parts, or decorative chrome work.

For a Sialkot-based finishing operation, a 1000 A rectifier is the workhorse for chrome, nickel, copper, zinc, and brass plating tanks. Output current is the direct driver of plating throughput — more amps means more parts processed per hour. Combined with a stable controllable voltage (typically 6–18 V depending on the bath chemistry), this rectifier supports a wide range of finishing recipes used across the surgical instrument and cutlery industry.
