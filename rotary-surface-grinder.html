About this Rotary Product Surface Grinder

A rotary product surface grinder is a high-throughput machine designed for grinding the flat faces of small workpieces — surgical instruments, cutting tools, automotive components, bearing rings — at production volumes that conventional reciprocating-table grinders cannot match. The rotary table allows continuous loading and unloading while the grinding wheel maintains contact with parts moving past, enabling typical outputs of 100–200 pieces per hour on small parts.

This particular unit is rated at 150 pieces per hour, placing it in the sweet spot for a Sialkot-style cutlery, surgical-instrument, or precision-tool shop. The fixed-position grinding head minimizes setup variance between batches, and the rotary table holds parts magnetically (or with custom fixturing) for high repeatability across thousands of parts. With a fine-grit ceramic wheel, surface finish under Ra 0.4 µm is consistently achievable.
